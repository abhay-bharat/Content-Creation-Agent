# Weekly Idea Capture Template

Use this template to capture 15 ideas weekly, filter to top 5, script top 3, and produce 2-3.

## Weekly Goals
- **Capture**: 15 ideas
- **Filter**: Top 5 (high quality + audience fit)
- **Script**: Top 3 of the 5
- **Produce**: 2-3 content pieces
- **Publish**: Follow weekly cadence (2 reels, 1 carousel, 3-5 AI Signals)
